ABOUT US



When I was a kid, I always want to eat and make my own cake that I will design a colorful design that I can use when my birthday come to blow .I'm a kind of kid before who want cakes even though I'm not into the taste of other cake but I'm more interested on how it represent to our eyes, how it is beautiful in our eyes. It always amaze and thinking of how they make it in short of time. Every birthday, every year, all i want is cake but due to financial problem, it will hard for us to buy and blow a cake or a simple cake on my birthday and my brother/my sisters birthday. There is a time that I'm crying just to convince my Mom and Dad to buy me a cake but they didn't granted it because they dont have that enough money and it's budgetable for our needs only and now as I grow older, I'm always dreaming about baking my own cake, putting it in a oven and designing it using the imagination on my minds, experementing how cakes taste and how will people react on it once I done making a cake. This is an reason on wheee I get the idea on how I build my own cafe, a cafe where I'm the one who bake the cake that have a lots kind of decorations. This idea come up to my mind and also to make my own recipe of pastry and desserts that people will like especially to those people who want sweets.
